BRIEF HISTORY

EXPERIENCE YOU CAN COUNT ON

Cube bio-Energy Private Limited (CBEPL) is the fastest growing company in Waste Management in India. CBEPL established in 2009 started its journey by providing solutions to Fecal Sludge Management and Bio methanation. CBEPL now focusing on Municipal Solid Waste, Industrial Waste Management, Biomedical Waste, Construction and Demolition Waste, Integrated Environmental Services, Consultancy and Recycling with complete end to end economically viable solutions.

At present CBEPL has foot prints, 85 locations spread across India. CBEPL is the pioneer in Fecal Sludge Management and Bio methanation. CBEPL manages 2.2 Mn tons of Municipal Solid Waste per annum, 1+ Lakhs KLD Fecal Sludge per annum and 75000 TPD of MRF per annum. CBEPL is having techno commercial solution partnerships with countries like, Peru, Belgium, Sweden, Germany, Italy, Singapore, Japan and USA.

CBEPL fully committed to ensuring its operations comply with all CPCB and other applicable laws to achieve ESG goals defined by UN. Over 12+ years of operational history, CBEPL has a competitive edge over all other companies, as CBEPL is open to provide economically viable techno commercial solutions to all kinds of small, medium and large-scale problems in waste management.
